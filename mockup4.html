Features

Everything you need
to remember every trip.

Six ways Pinr transforms how you document, share, and relive your travels.

  • 01

    Interactive 3D Globe

    Pin your travels on a beautiful, explorable 3D globe. Every destination becomes a permanent marker on your personal map.

  • 02

    Photo Stories

    Create rich photo stories linking multiple destinations. One trip, many places, a single beautiful narrative.

  • 03

    Social Discovery

    Connect with friends and see their adventures unfold in real time. Discover new destinations through the people you trust.

  • 04

    Geography Games

    Test your knowledge with Flag Dash, Pin Drop, and Travel Battle. Three game modes designed to make geography fun.

  • 05

    Leaderboards

    Challenge friends and compete for the top spot. Every game, every score, tracked and ranked across your network.

  • 06

    Smart Notifications

    Push notifications for friend activity, game invites, and new stories from the people you follow.
