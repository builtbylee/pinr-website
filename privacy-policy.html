Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pinr ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

1.1 Location Data

We request access to your device's location (access coarse location and access fine location) to show your current position on the global map and to tag your travel memories with the correct geographic coordinates.

1.2 Camera and Photos

We request access to your device's camera and photo library (read external storage, write external storage, read media images) to allow you to take photos or select existing images to create "Pins" and travel memories.

1.3 Microphone

We request access to record audio solely for the purpose of capturing audio notes if you choose to attach them to your memories. We do not listen to or record conversations in the background.

1.4 Sharing Controls

You have the control to allow or prevent your friends from sharing your pins and journeys. This preference can be managed in the Privacy section of the Settings menu.

1.5 Image Analysis & Content Moderation

To maintain a safe community and prevent the sharing of illegal or abusive content, we use automated systems (Sightengine) to analyze uploaded images for:

  • Nudity and adult content
  • Weapons and violence
  • Offensive symbols

Images flagged as violating our Terms of Service are strictly rejected and deleted immediately. We do not retain rejected content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (mapping, memory creation).
  • To personalize your experience by showing relevant content based on your location.
  • To improve our services and develop new features.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Sign-In & Firebase: For authentication and data storage.
  • Mapbox: For rendering maps and location services.
  • Sightengine: For automated image content moderation and safety analysis.
  • OneSignal: For delivering push notifications.
  • Expo: For application infrastructure.

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the in-app settings (Settings > Delete Account).

If you are unable to access the app, you may request account deletion by emailing lee@getpinr.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will verify your identity and delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
